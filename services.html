NATIONWIDE TRANSPORTATION

ProQuality Transportation provides expert and reliable transportation services throughout North America. We are able to meet our customers’ needs via our own fleet of trucks and network of reliable service providers.
ProQuality Transportation runs a fleet of brand new 124 reefer trucks operated by top professional drivers with years of experience. Our brokerage side works with motor carriers who must meet our strict requirements for making safe and on-time deliveries
Requirements for these carriers include:
  • FMCSA broker licensing
  • TWIC compliance
  • Customs bonding and documentation
  • Hazmat certification
  • DOT compliance
ProQuality Transportation expertly handles equipment types including:
  • Full Container
  • Dry Van
  • Reefer
  • LTL
Our professional approach ensures that no matter the size of the cargo, it is handled with precision, urgency, and care. We understand that transportation is a significant link in our client’s supply chain process and we take great care in our essential role.

WAREHOUSE LOGISTICS

ProQuality Transportation manages roughly 125,000 sq. ft. of warehouse storage space. We keep up with the essential needs of our customers through our networked systems which help to provide essential solutions.
We focus on technology which guarantees our ability to stay flexible and up-to-date, and our broad range of third-party logistics services includes:
  • Labeling
  • Reworking
  • Wrapping
  • Kitting
  • Palletizing
  • Packaging
  • Picking and Packing
  • Physical Inventory
  • Sub-assembly
Additionally, we are able to customize special projects by request from our clients because our vital solutions are tailored to fit each individual business.
If you or your company want to hear more about our services and are interested in potentially working with us, don’t hesitate to request a quote.

CROSS DOCKING

Cross Docking is a logistics procedure of unloading products and materials from an incoming supplier or manufacturer and loading them directly into outgoing trucks or trailers.
ProQuality Transportation offers this service for our clients to:
  • Optimize production time
  • Cut costs
  • Reduce inventory
We are utilizing the newest information technology available to allow our clients to monitor shipment status updates in real-time as well as all shipping activity records.
