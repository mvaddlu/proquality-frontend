About us

Our Facilities
In addition to our head office in Sumner, WA, ProQuality has a warehouse in Puyallup, WA offering 125,000 sq ft of dry warehouse space and abundant trailer parking space.
Our Mission
At ProQuality Transportation & Logistics Services, we are committed to making your supply chain more productive and profitable. We dive deep into your unique supply chain process, crafting tailored solutions specifically for your needs.
Innovation & Updates
We tirelessly strive to offer one-of-a-kind supply chain solutions and provide efficiency for your operation through constant innovation and updates. We also try to reduce the overall expense associated with the drayage process. Our import and export containers are located in the Seattle-Tacoma area.
Our Customers
We work with food manufacturers and shippers across 48 states, working hard to get fresh products from point A to point B in the safest and quickest manner.
We are proud to support small businesses locally in Washington and Oregon, as well as to be a partner of nationwide shippers and manufacturers.
